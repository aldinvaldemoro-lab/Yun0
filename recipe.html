Tiramisu

Creamy, coffee-soaked layers with mascarpone and a dusting of cocoa.

9×9″
12 servings
30 mins
6+ hours

Ingredients:

Coffee Soak

  • 1½ cups (360ml) strong espresso or dark coffee, cooled
  • 2 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • 2 tbsp almond extract (optional)

Mascarpone Filling

  • 4 large pasteurized egg yolks
  • ½ cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 16 oz (450g) mascarpone, room temperature
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream, chilled

Assembly

  • 24–30 hard ladyfingers (Savoiardi)
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder

Directions:

Prep the coffee soak:

  • Mix espresso, sugar and almond extract in a shallow dish. Set aside.

Make the filling:

  • Whisk egg yolks + sugar until pale and thick (~5 mins). Add vanilla.
  • Beat in mascarpone until smooth.
  • Whip heavy cream to stiff peaks, fold into mascarpone.

Assemble & Chill:

  • Quickly dip each ladyfinger in coffee (1–2 sec per side) — don’t oversoak.
  • Layer, spread filling, repeat, dust with cocoa.
  • Refrigerate at least 6 hours (overnight best).

Notes:

  • Use pasteurized eggs for safety.
  • Keep mascarpone room temp for smooth texture, but keep cream chilled until whipping.
  • Chill to let flavors meld.
