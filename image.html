SLAM DUNK

Slam Dunk (スラムダンク) is a classic basketball manga and anime series created by Takehiko Inoue. It follows the story of Hanamichi Sakuragi, a high school delinquent who joins the basketball team to impress a girl but soon discovers his passion for the sport. The series features intense basketball matches and memorable characters who grow both on and off the court.

JUJUTSU KAISEN

Jujutsu Kaisen (呪術廻戦) is a popular dark fantasy manga and anime series by Gege Akutami. It follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who joins a secret organization of Jujutsu Sorcerers to fight curses and protect humanity. The series is known for its intense battles, unique characters, and deep storylines.
